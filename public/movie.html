The Greatest Christmas Films of All Time

My top Christmas films of all time

Merry Christmas!
It is time to put up the Christmas decorations, eat mince pies, and drink Bailey’s. Most importantly, it is time once again to watch Christmas films.
Each year, I carefully analyse Christmas films and break them down into categories for your convenience.

But first, let me define what a Christmas film is: "A Christmas film is a cinematic work that centres its narrative, themes, or setting around Christmas. These films typically explore elements such as festive traditions, goodwill, family, leaping off skyscrapers with a hose tied around your waist, and the magic of Christmas."

In true High-Fidelity style, I have provided the top 5 Christmas films in a variety of categories. Get ready for a sleigh ride through the classics, the hilariously bad, the new gems, the soppy, and some bizarre ones. The lists are:

  • Essentials: The tried and true, it is not Christmas until you watch all of these.
  • So Bad They're Good: Because let's face it, corny is what we want from a Christmas film.
  • Animated films: Because everyone loves cartoons.
  • Soppy Christmas: Some people like these.
  • Alternative Christmas List: For those who like a little horror or a sprinkle of the unexpected with their Bailey’s.
  • New Films (Since 2021): Freshly unwrapped presents for your viewing pleasure.

So, I will provide a list of 30 Christmas films to get you into the spirit! This is my gift to you all!
Feel free to debate, discuss, and, most importantly, enjoy these festive flicks. May your Christmas be filled with laughter, joy, and hopefully, no arguments about whether Die Hard is a Christmas movie. Because it is. Get over it.
